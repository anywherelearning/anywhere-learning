Thanks for grabbing 7 Days of Real-World Learning. I made it for parents who already feel it: the best learning rarely looks like school. It looks like a kid counting out coins at the till, or planning a Saturday outing and actually being in charge of it.

Inside are seven small, hands-on activities, one for each real-world muscle: outdoor and nature, real-world math, creativity, AI and digital, entrepreneurship, communication, and planning. Each one fits into an afternoon, with no prep beyond what's already in your kitchen drawer.
