Hey!

Just checking in — did you get a chance to print your pack yet?

If it's still sitting in your downloads folder, no judgement. But here are three quick tips to make sure it actually gets used (and doesn't become one of those things you "meant to get to"):

**1. Print it and leave it out.**
Kitchen counter, coffee table, the middle of the living room floor — wherever your kids will see it. If it goes into a binder or a filing cabinet, it disappears. Visible = used.

**2. Let your kids choose.**
Don't assign an activity. Spread it out and let them pick. When kids choose what they do, they care more. That small bit of ownership changes everything.

**3. Follow the rabbit holes.**
If an activity sparks a question, a conversation, or a completely unexpected tangent — let them go down it. That's not getting off track. That's the whole point.
